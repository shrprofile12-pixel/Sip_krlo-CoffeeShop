Welcome to
Sip Krlo Coffee

At Sip Krlo Coffee, we believe that every sip should be an experience. From rich espressos to creamy lattes, we serve handcrafted beverages made with passion and the finest beans.

Our Story

Founded with the vision to bring excellence and innovation, our business has grown from a small idea into a vibrant enterprise. We are committed to delivering outstanding services and building lasting relationships with our customers. Our journey has been fueled by passion, dedication, and an unwavering pursuit of quality.

Our Mission

To provide top-notch services and solutions that exceed customer expectations, while fostering a culture of integrity, innovation, and sustainability.

Our Vision

To become a leading brand recognized for excellence, customer satisfaction, and positive community impact, driving progress and innovation in our industry.

Our Founder & Chief Happiness Officer

Mush Panjwani has been a sales, marketing, and training expert for over 35 years. He has worked with corporates, leaders, and entrepreneurs in 20 countries. Mush is also the author of Dhinchak Life, a book about living a fantastic life!

Mush launched SIP KRLO on 21st MAY 2025 with a dream of making it the best and the biggest coffee brand across Pakistan.

Mush is also a certified ‘happiness trainer’ and trains at corporates and non-profits. More about Mush at www.MushPanjwani.com
